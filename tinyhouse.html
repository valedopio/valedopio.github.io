QUINTA VALE DO PIO

The Stable House
Short Stay Rental

A one bedroom tiny house  converted from our original stable building available for short term rental for up to 2 adults and 2 children (with sofa bed).

The Interior

Living Room Space

Stylish sealed conscrete floors and big windows make this feel light and airy and wide open.

Kitchen Area

Thus compact kitchen has everything you need to create full meals to light snacks.

Shower Room

Everything in this room has been upcyled or found. From the river bebbles that make up the showertray or the recalimed windows and tiles. 

Bedroom

A comfortable double bed in the traditional Portuguese style makes for a good sleep and the opaque picture window works for a perfect late lay-in.
